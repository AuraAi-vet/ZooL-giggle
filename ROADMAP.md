### Week 1: Polish & Onboarding
- **Goal**: Ensure the first 5 minutes of the app experience are seamless.

    - [x] Implement the Onboarding Wizard.
    - [x] Finalize Appointment Booking.
    - [x] Add "Feedback" section to Settings.

### Week 2: Engagement & AI Refinement
- **Goal**: Keep users coming back and improve AI utility.
- **Tasks**:
    - [x] Implement Local Notifications for reminders.
    - [x] Add "Pet Milestones" (e.g., "Bruno is 6 months old today!").
    - [ ] Fine-tune AI prompts for better Indian-context veterinary advice.

### Week 3: Launch Readiness
- **Goal**: Technical hardening and documentation.
- **Tasks**:
    - Final Security Audit of Firestore Rules.
    - Performance optimization (Asset compression, code splitting).

### Week 4: Trial Launch
- **Goal**: Controlled release and feedback gathering.
- **Tasks**:
    - Invite first 50 beta testers.
    - Monitor error logs via console/analytics.
    - Weekly feedback synthesis and rapid bug fixes.

## 📈 Success Metrics for Trial
1. **Retention**: 40% of users return within 3 days.
2. **Engagement**: At least 2 AI queries per user per week.
3. **Utility**: At least 1 health record uploaded per pet.
4. **Feedback**: Minimum 10 constructive suggestions from the trial group.
